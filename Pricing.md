Ariya Pricing Model and Revenue Structure
Welcome to Ariya, the ultimate platform connecting event organizers with reliable vendors and providing essential planning tools. Our pricing model is designed to deliver excellent value to our customers, ensure competitive rates for our vendors, and create a sustainable revenue stream for Ariya. All prices are in Naira (₦).

1. Commission on Bookings
Ariya earns a commission from both customers and vendors for every successful booking made through the platform.
Customer Service Fee: 3% of the total booking amount (added at checkout).
Vendor Commission: 7% of the total booking amount (deducted from the vendor's payout).
Example: For a booking worth ₦100,000:
Customer pays: ₦103,000 (includes service fee).
Vendor receives: ₦93,000 (after commission deduction).
Ariya earns: ₦10,000 in total revenue (₦3,000 from customer, ₦7,000 from vendor).

2. Transaction Fees
Transaction fees apply to all payment activities conducted on the Ariya platform.
Applicable Transactions:
Event bookings
Registry purchases
Add-on services (e.g., priority support, upgrades)
Fee Structure:
Service Fee: 3% per transaction (paid by the customer).
Payment Processing Fee: Approximately 1.5% (This fee may be passed through to the user or absorbed by Ariya based on strategic decisions).
